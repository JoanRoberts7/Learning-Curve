Welcome to Kipaji Remolding

At Kipaji Remolding, our mission is simple yet profound: to empower talented youth, promote education, and uplift disadvantaged communities. Founded in March 2019 by husband-and-wife duo Bob and Joan, Kipaji Remolding has been a beacon of hope for those in need

Our Vision:

We envision a world where every young mind has the opportunity to shine, where education is accessible to all, and where communities thrive through sustainable initiatives.

A Journey of Impact:

What began as weekend mentorship classes in local schools soon blossomed into a community-wide initiative. Even in the face of financial constraints, we ran two successful food drives in 2020, providing essential sustenance to nearly 1000 households during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. Our commitment to positive change remains unwavering.
